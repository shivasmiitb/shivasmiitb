About Me

I am a PhD candidate at the Shailesh J. Mehta School of Management, IIT Bombay, with research interests in digital platforms, innovation, competitiveness, and strategic management. My work focuses on understanding how firms from emerging economies develop platform capabilities and compete in international markets. Drawing on experience in software engineering, management consulting, market intelligence, and business education, I combine academic rigor with practical insights into technology-enabled business transformation. My research has been presented at leading academic forums, including the India Strategy Conference and the Digital Platform Ecosystems Conference, and has benefited from my experience as a Visiting Fellow at the University of Cambridge Judge Business School.
